About Our School

SMA Negeri 16 Surabaya known as SIXTEEN has been established since 1975. The high school located on Jalan Prapen, Surabaya, is one of the favorite schools with the motto smart shine.

Vision & Mission

Vision

Excellence In The Quality Of Science And Technology And Imtaq, Rooted In The Nation's Culture And With An Environmental And Global Outlook

Mission

  • Implement and develop the education unit level curriculum in accordance with the needs of the times.
  • Facilitating students in the learning process to develop their potential.
  • Developing character: Competent, Excellent, Religious, Disciplined, Active, Sportive, Clean, Cool, Beautiful and Anti-drugs.
  • Developing a tradition of achievement and healthy competition at local, national and global levels.
